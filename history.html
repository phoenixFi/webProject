Early Beginnings

Astana, now known as Nur-Sultan, was founded in 1830 as a small town and has grown into a major metropolis.

Key Historical Events

  1. 1830: Founded as Akmolinsk
  2. 1961: Renamed Tselinograd during Soviet Era
  3. 1997: Became the capital of Kazakhstan
  4. 2019: Renamed Nur-Sultan in honor of the first president
